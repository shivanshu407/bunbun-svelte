# BunBunClothing

## Product Overview

**BunBunClothing** is a B2C ecommerce platform specializing in women's clothing and essentials. We offer a curated selection of sarees, saree shapewear, blouses, towels, and other clothing essentials — delivering quality traditional and everyday wear directly to customers across India.

---

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Limited Access to Quality Ethnic Wear Online
Many customers struggle to find reliable online stores for sarees and traditional clothing essentials that offer both quality and affordability.

**Solution:** BunBunClothing provides a curated, quality-assured collection of sarees and clothing essentials with detailed product images, accurate sizing, and genuine customer reviews.

---

### Problem 2: Fragmented Shopping for Saree Essentials
Customers often need to visit multiple stores to buy sarees along with matching blouses, shapewear, and accessories.

**Solution:** One-stop shop for all saree-related needs — sarees, ready-made blouses, shapewear, and essentials, making outfit completion convenient.

---

### Problem 3: Confusing Checkout & Payment Experience
Many smaller ecommerce sites have complicated checkout flows and limited payment options for Indian customers.

**Solution:** Streamlined checkout with Razorpay integration supporting UPI, cards, net banking, and EMI — familiar and trusted by Indian shoppers.

---

### Problem 4: Lack of Order Visibility
Customers often don't know when their order will arrive or how to track it.

**Solution:** Real-time order tracking with SMS/email notifications at every stage — from order confirmation to delivery.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Women aged 25-55, primarily in India
- **Demographics:** Middle to upper-middle-class households
- **Behavior:** Comfortable shopping online, seeking quality ethnic wear at reasonable prices
- **Geography:** Tier 1, 2, and 3 cities in India
